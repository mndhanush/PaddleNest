Payment FAQs

Do you require a deposit?

For rentals, we place a $100 temporary hold on your credit card for incidentals. For guided tours, payment is collected in full at the time of booking.

What is your refund policy?

Cancel 48+ hours in advance for a 100% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours receive a 50% credit. Weather-related cancellations by PaddleNest are always fully refunded.

Are there any hidden booking fees?

No. The price you see includes all taxes, safety equipment, and transaction fees. Gratuity for your guide is entirely optional.
