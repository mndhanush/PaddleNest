Rental FAQs

Do I need a roof rack to rent a board?

No. We can provide soft racks that fit 95% of 4-door vehicles, or we can deliver the boards directly to your launch location.

What if I damage the equipment?

Normal wear and tear is expected. For major damage (punctures, broken paddles), an optional $10 damage waiver covers up to $500 in repairs.

Can I rent overnight?

Yes, our Full-Day rental covers 24 hours. You can keep the board overnight securely.
