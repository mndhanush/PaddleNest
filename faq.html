Safety & Gear.

Do I have to wear a life jacket?

Yes. Coast Guard regulations and PaddleNest policy strictly require all participants to wear a provided, fastened PFD (Personal Flotation Device) at all times while on the water. No exceptions.

What is an ankle leash?

An ankle leash tethers you to your paddleboard. In the event you fall off, wind or currents can quickly push the board away from you. The leash ensures your board (which acts as a giant floatation device) is always within reach.

Can you strap a rental board to my car?

Yes. We provide heavy-duty soft racks that fit 95% of standard 4-door vehicles. Our staff will securely strap the board to your roof before you leave HQ.

Clear, transparent rules regarding your money and reservations.

Cancellation & Refunds

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before your scheduled start time receive a 100% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours receive a 50% credit toward a future booking. No-shows will not be refunded.

Weather Cancellations

If PaddleNest cancels your reservation due to unsafe weather conditions (high winds, lightning, marine advisories), you will automatically receive a 100% refund or the option to reschedule for free.

Late Returns (Rentals)

A grace period of 30 minutes is allowed. After that, late returns are billed at $20 per hour. If you anticipate being late, please contact HQ immediately.

Damage & Lost Gear

Normal wear (scratches) is expected. Lost paddles, deep punctures, or snapped fins will be billed at repair/replacement cost. An optional $10 damage waiver at checkout covers up to $500 in accidental damage.
