Policies & Preparation

The PaddleNest
Code.

To ensure a safe, sustainable, and unforgettable adventure, we ask all explorers to adhere to our strict guidelines before hitting the water.

Read Guidelines
Non-Negotiable

The Golden Rules

Your safety is our absolute priority. These rules apply to both guided tours and independent rentals.

Always Wear a PFD

Personal Flotation Devices are mandatory. You must wear and securely fasten your provided Coast Guard-approved PFD at all times while on the water.

Stay Attached

Your ankle leash is your lifeline to the board. It must remain securely fastened to your ankle or calf for the entire duration of the expedition.

Follow the Guide

During guided tours, the lead instructor's word is law. Follow their route, maintain the set pace, and adhere to their safety signals instantly.

Zero Impact
Environmental Duty

Respect the Locals.

We are visitors in these ecosystems. Our goal is to leave the waterways exactly as we found them, ensuring they thrive for future generations.

  • Maintain Distance

    Stay at least 50 yards away from all marine wildlife. Do not chase, feed, or attempt to touch animals under any circumstances.

  • No Single-Use Plastics

    Plastic water bottles, wrappers, and grocery bags are strictly prohibited on our boards. Bring reusable thermoses only.

  • Reef-Safe Sunscreen Only

    Chemical sunscreens destroy marine life. Ensure your sunblock is mineral-based (zinc oxide or titanium dioxide) and marked Reef-Safe.

Preparation

The Packing List

Essentials

  • Reusable Water Bottle (Full)
  • Sunglasses (With a strap)
  • UV-Protective Rash Guard
  • Water Shoes or Secure Sandals
  • Change of dry clothes (Leave in car)

Prohibited

  • Glass Containers of any kind
  • Alcohol or Intoxicants
  • Loud Bluetooth Speakers
  • Valuable Jewelry or loose electronics
  • Single-use plastic bags
Weather Policy

Mother Nature is
The Boss.

We monitor coastal weather stations constantly. Expeditions go out in light rain, but we draw a strict line at unsafe conditions.

High Winds & Lightning

Tours are automatically canceled if sustained winds exceed 15 knots or if lightning is detected within 10 miles. You will receive a full refund or free reschedule.

Cancellation Window

Need to bail? We require 48 hours notice for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours receive a 50% credit toward future bookings.
