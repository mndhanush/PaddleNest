Know Your Limits

Route Categories

We classify our waterways based on current strength, wind exposure, and necessary endurance.

Level 1: Serene

Flat water, protected bays, and no currents. Perfect for beginners and families.

Wind: 0-5 Knots

Level 2: Coastal

Mild currents, light chop, and open waterways. Requires basic balancing skills.

Wind: 5-12 Knots

Level 3: Offshore

Open ocean swells, unpredictable wind, strong tides. Expert endurance required.

Wind: 12+ Knots

What to Expect

Anatomy of an Expedition

1. Briefing

Arrive at the dock. We handle safety rules, weather checks, and gear fitting.

2. Launch

Hit the water. The first 15 minutes are dedicated to pacing and balance checks.

3. The Apex

Reach the route's highlight. Time for snacks, rest, and professional photography.

4. Return

Navigate back utilizing the turning tide. Equipment drop-off and photo sharing.
