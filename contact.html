Quick Answers

Weather Policies

If adverse weather conditions prevent your launch, we offer full rescheduling or a 100% refund up to 2 hours prior to departure.

Group Bookings

We accommodate groups of up to 24 paddlers. For parties larger than 8, please contact us directly to arrange specialized guides.

Gear Returns

All gear must be returned to the original launch site unless a one-way expedition was pre-arranged with our dispatch team.

Corporate & Partnerships

Looking to host a team-building retreat or collaborate on an eco-friendly brand activation? We build custom itineraries tailored for professional groups and environmental initiatives.
